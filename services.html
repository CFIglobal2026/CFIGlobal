NRI Banking

Banking Solutions for Non-Resident Indians

We offer a complete suite of NRI banking services — helping you manage your finances in India from anywhere in the world, with full regulatory compliance.

NRE — Non-Resident External

A rupee account for parking foreign earnings in India. Interest is fully repatriable and tax-exempt. Ideal for transferring overseas income.

NRO — Non-Resident Ordinary

For managing income earned in India — rent, dividends, pensions. Repatriation allowed up to USD 1 million per year with proper documentation.

PIS — Portfolio Investment Scheme

Facilitates purchase and sale of shares and debentures of Indian companies on a stock exchange under the RBI's PIS framework.
